StardewSync
Save synchronization application for Stardew Valley

StardewSync is an application to power Stardew Valley with "cross-save" abilities.

It works like this: you download the application for PC and, for example, phone. Then you upload the save to the server with this application. It gives you magic words (MD5 hash), which you can paste to the app on another device. App will download your save from the server. Finally, you can play your save on your mobile phone.
